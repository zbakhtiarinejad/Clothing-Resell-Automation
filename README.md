
         CLOTHING RESELLING SPREADSHEET SYSTEM - OVERVIEW


WHAT IS THIS PROJECT?
This is an automated, macro-free management system built in Microsoft 
Excel designed to help you run, track, and scale your clothing reselling 
business smoothly up to 1,200+ active items.

WHAT IS IT USED FOR?
1. Automatic Profit Tracking: You just type in the SKU and the sale 
   price. The sheet automatically calculates your exact Net Profit by 
   instantly figuring out item costs, platform fees (eBay, Vinted, Depop), 
   and postage.

2. Live Business Dashboard: Gives you a real-time, bird's-eye view of 
   your total revenue, actual net profit, and exactly how much cash 
   you currently have tied up in unsold inventory.

3. Smart Sales Metrics: Automatically calculates your "Sell-Through Rate" 
   (how fast your items are clearing) and "Days to Sell" (how long items 
   sit before selling).

4. Better Buying Decisions: Automatically tracks and ranks your best 
   and worst-performing clothing categories and suppliers, so you know 
   exactly what inventory makes you the most money.

HOW TO USE IT IN 2 STEPS:
1. Log new items you buy in the 'Inventory' tab.
2. Log items when they sell in the 'Sales Log' tab. The rest is 100% 
   automated for you!
